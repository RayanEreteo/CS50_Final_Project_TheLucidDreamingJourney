Introduction


Welcome to "The Lucid Dreaming Journey".
In this page you will find information about the commonly used method to induce a lucid dream.

As a beginner, it can be hard to find which method to use, so let me show you a table of the commonly used technique and if they are for you.

Name Description Difficulty
Dream Journaling Involve writing your dreams to improve dream recall, also useful to notice dream signs that may trigger lucidity. Very Easy
Reality Checks The fundamental and most important method, reality checks. Involve literally checking if one is dreaming by being aware and testing your reality. Very Easy
MILD (Mnemonic Induced Lucid Dream) Developed by Dr.Stephen Laberge. This method is one of the most commonly practiced lucid dream induction techniques out there and involve good prospective memory and intention. Easy
WILD (Wake Initiated Lucid Dream) Developed by Dr.Stephen Laberge. This technique involve to keep the perfect balance between consciousness and falling asleep and can be hard to do for beginner. Medium / Hard
